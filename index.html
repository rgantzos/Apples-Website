About Apples

Apples is a super cool bot for your Discord server. We like to think of him as the "robot form of the everything bagel". What does that mean exactly? Well, we're not really sure. He just has a lot of features. The best part is that we're actively adding more.

Not quite convinced yet? Apples will welcome your members for you (if you enable the feature) in whatever channel you want him to. You can also easily toggle XP with a single command, as well as easily give XP to the users you want to give it to. Yes, it really is that simple.
